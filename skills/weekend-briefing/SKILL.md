# /weekend-briefing — Weekend Lightweight Briefing

Execute these steps in order. Complete in <500 output tokens. No MCP calls, no email, no calendar, no jobs.

## Step 1: Read Local Files
Read these files in parallel:
- `tracking/session-state.md` — carry-forward items, pipeline health, nag counters
- `goals/quarterly.md` — non-revenue quarterly goals
- `tracking/certifications.md` — study progress (if active)
- `tracking/fitness.md` — gym counter for the week
- `references/open-source-strategy.md` — current stage and next steps

Also scan CLAUDE.md "Skills Backlog" section for buildable items.

## Step 2: Build Task List
From the files above, assemble 3-5 actionable non-revenue items. Sources:
- Carry-forward items from session-state.md that can be prepped on weekends
- Open source strategy next steps
- Skills backlog items from CLAUDE.md
- Quarterly goals with non-revenue focus (certifications, tools, personal)

For each item, estimate effort: quick (30min), medium (1-2h), deep (half-day).

## Step 3: Fill Template
Fill `templates/weekend-briefing.md` with:
- Pipeline Reminder: one line with hottest lead + next action + deadline (stays top of mind)
- Build & Non-Revenue Tasks: the 3-5 items from Step 2
- Carry-Forward: items from session-state.md relevant for weekend prep
- Health: gym counter for the week

No reactivation contacts — targets are not working weekends.
No email/calendar pulls — keep it instant.
No task re-ranking — weekend is self-directed.
