## 1. Contact Segments

Partition your contacts into functional segments. Each segment has its own cadence, tone, and value proposition.

Example segments:

- **Active leads:** deals in any stage from contacted to negotiating
- **Warm network:** former colleagues, former clients, past referrers
- **Recruiters:** both preferred and to-avoid, with notes on what they deliver
- **Personal:** friends, family, non-business contacts
- **Dormant:** 90+ days silent, not yet archived
